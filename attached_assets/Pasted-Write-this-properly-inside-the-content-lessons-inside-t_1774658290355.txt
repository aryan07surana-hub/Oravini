🔥 THE CONTENT TRIAD (VIEWS → CONNECTION → TRUST)
Every piece of content you create should fall into one of these 3 buckets:
1. Views (Attention)
This is what gets people in.
Transformation videos
Day-in-the-life content
Rapid-fire edits
Reaction videos
Trend-based clips
👉 Goal: Stop the scroll

2. Connection (Relatability)
This is what makes people stay.
B-roll storytelling
Raw talking head videos
Podcast-style clips
Voiceover + visuals
Personal experiences
👉 Goal: Make them feel understood

3. Trust (Authority)
This is what makes people buy.
Educational (green screen) content
Coaching call breakdowns
Live work sessions
Case studies
Proof-based content
👉 Goal: Make them believe you can help them

⚡ CONTENT → BUSINESS PIPELINE
Content → gets you views
Story → builds connection
Value → builds trust
CTA → generates leads
DMs → convert to sales

🎯 FORMAT BREAKDOWN (WHAT EACH TYPE DOES)
🔹 Views Formats
Transformation content → humans are wired to follow progress
Reaction videos → fast dopamine
Rapid-fire edits → high retention
⚠️ Problem: High views, low conversion if not structured properly

🔹 Connection Formats
Raw talking head (unscripted)
B-roll + storytelling
Podcast clips
👉 These build emotional attachment

🔹 Trust Formats
Educational breakdowns
Coaching calls
Live execution videos
👉 These build authority and credibility

🧠 RAW TALKING HEAD TRUTH
Raw, unscripted talking head videos:
Build deep trust
Feel authentic
But don’t go viral easily at the start
👉 Recommendation:
 Only double down on this after you’ve built consistency
 (roughly 6–24 months of posting)
