Step 1: Start Audit

Ask things like:

What platform? (Instagram / YouTube)
Niche
Target audience
Biggest struggle
Goals (growth / monetisation / brand deals)

“Insert your Instagram URL”

Backend API call
Pull last 30 posts data
Start AI analysis
Step 3: Processing Screen (30–60 sec)

“Analyzing your content…”

Step 4: Report Delivery
Show preview of report
DO NOT give full access instantly
Step 5: CTA After Report
“Upgrade to unlock full report”
“Get deeper insights + strategy”

❌ DO NOT show “buy extra credits” (for now)
