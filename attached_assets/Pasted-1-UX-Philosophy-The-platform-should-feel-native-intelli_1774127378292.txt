1. UX Philosophy
The platform should feel native, intelligent, and seamless.
Do not expose APIs, providers, or backend services (e.g., Gemini, Fireworks, etc.) anywhere in the UI.
All AI actions should appear as built-in intelligence, not third-party integrations.
Maintain a processing delay of ~25–30 seconds for major AI operations to:
Build perceived depth and intelligence
Avoid “instant = low quality” perception
Increase user trust in output quality
2. AI Content Generator
