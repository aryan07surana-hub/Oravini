## Pro Tips

### For All Tools
- ✅ Be specific (not "I help people" but "I help busy moms lose 20lbs")
- ✅ Use numbers in social proof (500+ clients, $1M+ generated)
- ✅ Keep CTAs clear and single-action
- ✅ Test multiple versions (A/B test on your profile)
- ✅ Update bio every 30-60 days

### Generate from Scratch
- Fill in ALL fields for best results
- Use your onboarding niche (pre-filled)
- Be hyper-specific in "what you do"
- Include real numbers in social proof
- Mix and match lines to find perfect combo

### Improve Existing Bio
- Paste your EXACT current bio
- Review score breakdown carefully
- Focus on lowest-scoring areas
- Compare "what changed" to understand improvements
- Run multiple times with different platforms

### Template Selector
- Choose template closest to your actual role
- Don't leave fields blank (AI needs context)
- Customize the filled template before polishing
- Try multiple templates to compare
- Use examples as inspiration, not copy

### Competitor Analysis
- Use 3-5 bios (not just 2)
- Pick SUCCESSFUL competitors (high engagement)
- Paste their FULL bios
- Review patterns carefully
- Don't copy - use insights to differentiate

## Character Limits

| Platform | Limit | Strategy |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Instagram | 150 chars | Be concise, use emojis sparingly, focus on hook + CTA |
| Twitter/X | 160 chars | Similar to Instagram, slightly more room |
| LinkedIn | 220 chars | More professional, can include credentials |

**Over the limit?**
- Remove filler words
- Use numbers instead of words (500+ not "five hundred")
- Shorten CTA
- Remove emojis
- Use abbreviations (CEO not Chief Executive Officer)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Too vague**: "I help people grow" → ✅ "I help coaches hit $10K/mo"
❌ **No CTA**: Just describing yourself → ✅ Clear next step
❌ **Too long**: 200+ characters → ✅ Under platform limit
❌ **No social proof**: Just claims → ✅ Numbers and results
❌ **Generic**: Could apply to anyone → ✅ Niche-specific
❌ **Multiple CTAs**: "DM me, book a call, download..." → ✅ One clear action
❌ **No link**: Missing the 4th line → ✅ Always include link

## Bio Framework (4-Line Structure)

### Line 1: Attract/Repel
**Purpose**: Filter your ideal audience
**Formula**: "I help [specific audience] [specific outcome] without [common pain]"
**Example**: "I help busy moms lose 20lbs without giving up wine"

### Line 2: Social Proof
**Purpose**: Build credibility
**Formula**: "[Number] [result] | [credential/achievement]"
**Example**: "500+ transformations | Featured in Women's Health"

### Line 3: Call to Action
**Purpose**: Tell them what to do next
**Formula**: "[Action verb] [specific thing]"
**Example**: "Get my free meal plan below 👇"

### Line 4: Link
**Purpose**: Drive traffic
**Formula**: "[URL or link-in-bio]"
**Example**: "linktr.ee/yourname"

## Optimization Checklist

Before finalizing your bio, check:

- [ ] Under character limit for your platform
- [ ] Hyper-specific (not generic)
- [ ] Includes numbers/social proof
- [ ] Clear CTA (one action)
- [ ] Link included
- [ ] No typos
- [ ] Reads naturally when spoken aloud
- [ ] Differentiates you from competitors
- [ ] Speaks to your ideal client
- [ ] Updated within last 60 days

## When to Update Your Bio

Update your bio when:
- ✅ You hit a new milestone (1000 followers, 100 clients, etc.)
- ✅ You change your offer/positioning
- ✅ Your current bio is 60+ days old
- ✅ You're not getting the results you want
- ✅ You launch a new product/service
- ✅ Your niche evolves
- ✅ Competitor analysis reveals better positioning
